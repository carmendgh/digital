The brief
B&Q needed a new careers site that would fully communicate their new Employer Brand and position them as a modern, digital retailer with a dedicated and diverse workforce. It was also important to attract more informed candidates who could effectively self-select, improving the volume to quality application ratio.

As an additional challenge, we had very limited access to new content such as imagery, due to the site being created during the pandemic. This meant we had to make the most of existing assets, and even integrate user-generated content from employees.
Gathering insights
We kicked off with a strategy meeting, where we jointly reviewed user surveys and existing website data, and narrowed down the scope of work.

Based on our research, we found out the following insights:

The solution
We decided to dynamically aggregate content to ensure candidates engage with relevant brand messaging whatever their route to application. Using our own smart careers site technology, we created a highly personalised user experience that would help candidates discover their own version of 'Together We Can'.

We avoided soul-destroying searches, clicking through irrelevant content and boring job descriptions, and added some slick site design, crafted copy and tons of tailored content that would immerse them in B&Qâ€™s culture.
